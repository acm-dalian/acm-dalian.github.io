Call for Papers:ICCCS 2015

2014年10月09日 13:12

Welcome to ICCCS2015

         2015 International Conference on Cloud Computing and Security (ICCCS 2015) will be held on August 13-15, 2015 in Nanjing, China. The objective of ICCCS 2015 is to provide a forum for researchers, academicians, engineers, industrial professionals, students and government officials involved in the general areas of information security and cloud computing, etc. to disseminate their latest research results and exchange views on the future research directions of these fields.

Important Dates

         Paper submission deadline June 10, 2015
Author notification July 10, 2015
Final manuscript and Registration due July 20, 2015

Call for paper/span>

         The conference focuses on all aspects of cloud computing and security. The topics of interests include, but not limited to: Cloud Computing Architecture and Systems.
Cloud Resource Virtualization and Management.
Security and Privacy in Cloud Computing.
Business vs. Software on a Cloud.
Green Computing Models, Simulations, Designs, and Paradigms.
Mobile Applications.
Wireless and Mobile Networks.
Cloud and Networks Security.
Information Security and Risks Management.
Information Retrieval, Data Mining.
Multimedia Web-Based Communities and Applications.
Big Data Management, Processing, and Visualization.
Enterprise Architecture, and Systems.
Information System Applications (E-Government, E-Learning, E-Health, E-Commerce).

Publication

         All the accepted papers will be published as a post-proceedings in Springer-Verlag's Lecture Notes in Computer Science (LNCS) series.
